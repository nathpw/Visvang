## After Downloading

1. Place files in the correct `Assets/Audio/` subfolders
2. Open Unity → files auto-import
3. Select the `AudioManager` GameObject in the scene hierarchy
4. Drag-and-drop audio clips into the corresponding slots in the Inspector
5. OR use the `AudioAssetLoader` script to auto-assign by filename convention
